# Positioning and Messaging

## One-Line Value Proposition
Cut recurring client-delivery operations time in 30 days with auditable AI workflow automation.

## Core Job-To-Be-Done
Automate recurring client delivery work (reporting, approvals, handoff) with run history and workspace-level isolation.

## Messaging Rules
- Lead with outcomes, not protocol names.
- Lead with one buyer persona: agency operations lead.
- Show reliability and traceability in every demo.
- De-emphasize broad "platform" language until after pilot proof.

## Homepage/Deck Headline Options
- "Ship recurring client deliverables faster with auditable AI workflows."
- "From manual ops to repeatable client delivery in 6 weeks."
- "Agency operations automation with approval gates and run history built in."
