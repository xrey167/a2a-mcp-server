# 6-Week Pilot Playbook

## Week 1
- Baseline workflow and KPI capture
- Workspace setup and permissions
- Pick one recurring client-delivery process

## Week 2
- Deploy first template workflow
- Validate run history and audit trail
- Establish approval gate owner

## Week 3-4
- Expand to second template if stable
- Weekly KPI review: runs completed, failure rate, estimated hours saved
- Tune prompts/steps and retry policies

## Week 5
- Prepare executive summary with before/after metrics
- Validate expansion scope

## Week 6
- Final ROI review and recommendation
- Convert to recurring monthly subscription
